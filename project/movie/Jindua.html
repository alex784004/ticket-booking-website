SYNOPSIS

The film belongs to this character KARMA (Jimmy) who lives in Canada and about his journey of in-between days from Kachha (illegal) to Pakka (legal), with a twist of love-triangle. In his journey, he meets these two characters, who are the leading female protagonists of the film. EESH (Neeru), who is a Canadian citizen. As she is brought up in Canada, her mentality is western and wants to make life's decisions by herself, but she has only one problem in her life, that is, her father is always poking her to get married soon to a Punjabi guy. SAGHI (Sargun) is the other lead protagonist female. She is basically from Punjab. She came to Canada on a study visa.

LEAD CAST

Neeru Bajwa
Jimmy Shergill
Sargun Mehta
