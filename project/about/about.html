Holokart

Holokart.com is an online movie ticketing website founded by four persons (Tejas Rana, Subham Singh, Himanshu Maimia and Vaibhav Sharma). Holokart.com provides movie times for all theatres, and online ticket purchasing for all National Amusements theatres and all Clearview Cinemas, among other smaller chains, such as Carnival theatres situated at various place. Providing advance ticketing services to 250 theatres chains.
